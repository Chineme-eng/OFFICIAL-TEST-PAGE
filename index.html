About Me

Hey stranger, I'm Kat or KP. I build digital things: web tools that save time, templates that look great out of the box, guides that actually teach, and startups that scratch real itches.

Most of my work are decided bydesign and utility. I'm a huge nerd that loves when things are sharp, focused and do exactly what they are suposed to.

Everything here started with a problem I personally had. From Picao AI to calculators, tax tools, and coding guides, it all comes from the same instinct: make useful things and make them feel good to use.
